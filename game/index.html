Welcome to Simple Acey Deucey
The goal of the game is to predict whether your card will be between the dealer's two cards.
  • Aces are high
  • If you believe the next card is between the dealt cards, click bet.
  • If you believe it is outside, pass.
  • If your card matches one of the dealt cards you lose double your bet.
  • Normally you could vary you bet amount based on your confidence, but that is too complicated for this exercise
  • Single deck
  • Deck is reshuffled before each game
  • Suits are not displayed, but there are 4 of every value
